EXPLANATION

Moviethings

This web project was created as a personal movie tracking tool. It allows me to log movies I’ve watched, store personal ratings, and keep a history of all the films I’ve seen. The goal is to help me easily remember which movies I’ve already watched and how I rated them. It also serves as a way to reflect on my movie-watching journey over time.
